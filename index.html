Utah, United States

Utah is a state in the western United States known for its dramatic landscapes, including deserts, canyons, mountains, and five national parks. It is famous for outdoor recreation such as hiking, skiing, and rock climbing, as well as for the Great Salt Lake. Utah’s capital and largest city is Salt Lake City, and the state has a strong cultural heritage influenced by Native American history and the early settlement of members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
